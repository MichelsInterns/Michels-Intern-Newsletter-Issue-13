COMPANY SPOTLIGHT
What does Michels Trenchless Pty. Ltd. do?
Michels Trenchless Pty. Ltd. Is changing the game when it comes to underground construction in Australia.

With advanced techniques like Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) and Direct Pipe®, Michels Trenchless Pty. Ltd. uses trenchless technology in areas where traditional open-cut construction would not be practical or possible.
Company News: No-Dig Award
Michels won the 2025 ISTT No-Dig Award for the Snowy 2.0 Marica Road West HDD project in New South Wales, Australia.

This groundbreaking installation achieved a 563-meter (1,847-foot) elevation change—a first in HDD history.

Congratulations to our international team!

Quick Rest Ideas for the Long Weekend
As we head into Thanksgiving break, we hope you get a little extra time to breathe, unwind, and recharge. Here are a few simple ways to reset over the weekend—no big plans required.
☕ Take a Slow Morning
Skip the alarm if you can. Make a warm drink, open a window, and enjoy a slow start to your day.
🌲 Get Fresh Air
A quick walk outside—even in cold weather—does wonders for energy, creativity, and stress.
🧣 Do Something Cozy
Light a candle, watch a comfort show, bake something easy, or pull out your favorite hoodie. Lean into the fall vibes.
💬 Check in with Someone You Care About
A quick call or text to a friend or family member can make the weekend feel a little more connected.
